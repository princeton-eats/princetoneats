Princeton Eats!

Imagine this...

You’re rushing out of COS 333 at 12:20 PM, stomach growling, with just over an hour before your next class. You need to grab lunch, but there’s a problem—your dietary restrictions mean you can’t just walk into any dining hall and eat anything available. Princeton Dining’s website has the info, but sifting through menus for all seven halls? That’s time you don’t have, and you are NOT the only one who feels that way. It’s so many of us out here. That’s where Princeton Eats comes in, your personal dining meal recommend. In seconds, it scans every dining hall menu, filters meals based on your preferences—vegan, halal, gluten-free, nut-free, you name it—and tells you exactly where to go for a meal that fits your needs.

No stress, no scrolling, just fast, personalized meal recommendations.
